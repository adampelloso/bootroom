# Glossary

Quick reference for common terms used across the project.

- **BTTS**: Both Teams To Score (yes/no).
- **SOT**: Shots on Target.
- **xG**: Expected Goals.
- **1H / 2H**: First half / Second half.
- **Team total**: Goals scored by one team (home or away).
- **Total goals**: Combined goals scored by both teams.
- **Double chance**: 1X (home win or draw), 12 (home or away win), X2 (away win or draw).
- **Draw no bet**: Wager void on draw; settles on home/away result.
- **Corners total**: Combined corners by both teams.
- **Most corners**: Which team wins the corner count.
- **Shots diff**: Shots for minus shots against.
- **SOT diff**: Shots on target for minus shots on target against.
- **Rolling window (L5/L10)**: Last 5 or last 10 matches.
