# Market List (MVP)

Canonical list of the 21 markets we support at launch.  
All insights in the feed and detail views must map to one of these.

## Match markets (17)
1. Match result (1X2)
2. Double chance (1X / 12 / X2)
3. Draw no bet
4. Total goals (O/U 0.5, 1.5, 2.5, 3.5)
5. Team totals (home/away O/U 0.5, 1.5, 2.5)
6. BTTS yes/no
7. Correct score bands / clustering
8. 1H result (1X2)
9. 1H total goals (O/U 0.5 / 1.5)
10. 2H total goals (O/U 0.5 / 1.5)
11. Higher scoring half
12. Win either half
13. Total corners (O/U 8.5 / 9.5 / 10.5)
14. Team corners (home/away O/U)
15. Most corners
16. Team shots
17. Team shots on target

## Player markets (4)
18. Anytime goalscorer
19. Player shots
20. Player shots on target
21. Player assists
