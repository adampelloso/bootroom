# Market List (MVP)

Canonical list of the 21 markets we support at launch.  
All insights in the feed and detail views must map to one of these.

## Surfaced markets (UI focus)

We surface **five** bettor-trusted markets in the feed and match detail:

1. **Total goals in a match** – O/U (e.g. O2.5 hit rate + avg), total goals chart.
2. **Individual team totals** – Home/away goals (L5 home / L5 away) for team O/U context.
3. **Total corners in a match** – Combined corners (avg, last 10), chart.
4. **Team corners** – Corners for/against per team (home/away).
5. **Player shots and shots on target props** – Player-level Shots and SOT (match stats, post-match).

All other markets below remain in data and can be used for insights or future UI.
